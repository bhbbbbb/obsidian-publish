
## Lagrange Multiplier

- Statement &mdash; maximize $f(x, y)$ which is subject to $g(x, y) = 0$


<img src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/b/bf/LagrangeMultipliers2D.svg/450px-LagrangeMultipliers2D.svg.png" style="background-color: white;">

with the concept showed in the image, we can say that

$$
\begin{gather}
\nabla f \parallel \nabla g
\\\\
\implies
\nabla f = \lambda \nabla g
\end{gather}
$$

in which $\lambda$ is known as **Lagrange multiplier**.
